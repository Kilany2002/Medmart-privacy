# Privacy Policy for MedMart

**Last Updated: December 25, 2025**

MedMart ("we", "us", or "our") values your trust and is dedicated to being transparent about how we handle the details you share with us. This policy explains how we use your information to provide our services through the mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information You Provide to Us

To use MedMart effectively and process your orders, you provide us with certain details:

### 1.1 Account Details
When you create an account, you share:
- **Your Name:** To personalize your experience.
- **Contact Details:** Email address and phone number for account security and communication.
- **Clinic/Business Information:** Your clinic name and professional address.

### 1.2 Delivery Details
When placing an order, you provide:
- **Shipping Address:** Street, building, floor, apartment, city, and governorate to ensure your supplies reach the correct destination.
- **Special Instructions:** Any specific notes you add for our delivery team.

### 1.3 Order History
We keep a record of your purchases to help you manage your inventory, reorder items easily, and view your payment history.

## 2. How Your Details are Used

The information you share is used exclusively to:
- Provide and maintain your account.
- Process and ship your dental supplies.
- Send you order updates and essential service-related messages.
- Provide professional support when you need help.
- Ensure the safety and security of your transactions.

## 3. Storage and Security

Your information is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud infrastructure. We use modern security practices to keep your details safe. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers

To complete your orders, we securely share necessary details with:
- **Delivery Partners:** Only the address and contact info needed to deliver your items.
- **Payment Processors:** To securely handle your transactions.
- **Infrastructure Providers:** Secured systems (like Supabase) that host the app's functionality.

## 5. Your Preferences and Control

### 5.1 Updating Your Info
You can view and update your profile details at any time in the "Profile" section of the App.

### 5.2 Account Removal
If you choose to stop using MedMart, you can request to delete your account through the Profile settings. This will remove your personal details from our active systems, subject to legal record-keeping requirements for past transactions.

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may refresh this policy to reflect changes in our services. We will always post the latest version in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding how your details are handled, please reach out via the support section in the App.
